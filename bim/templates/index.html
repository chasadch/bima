Formatting Guide

To successfully map Revit quantities, ensure your CSV contains headers matching these keywords:

  • Material Name: To detect bricks, concrete, AAC, EPS, single/double/triple glazing.
  • Volume: Volume of structural block components (m³).
  • Area: Area of windows or insulation boards (m²).
Tip: Columns like "Material: Name", "Material: Volume", and "Material: Area" generated in standard Revit Material Takeoff schedules work perfectly!
